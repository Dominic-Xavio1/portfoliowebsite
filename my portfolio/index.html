Engineer of Ideas, Explorer of Reality

I’m Dominique Savio—a passionate developer and aspiring physicist with a relentless curiosity to understand how the world works and how to make it better. From a young age, I’ve been captivated by the invisible forces that govern our universe and the power of technology to reshape our lives.

My love for building goes beyond code. I find deep joy in creating systems that solve real-world problems—whether it’s through a clean user interface, a well-architected backend, or a clever fusion of scientific ideas and software logic. I believe in tools that not only work, but feel right. My approach to every project is guided by logic, empathy, and a strong desire to leave a mark on people’s lives.

I enjoy exploring physics experiments, reverse-engineering broken electronics, and turning ideas into working machines. This hands-on mindset feeds into my development work, where I constantly seek better, faster, and smarter solutions.

When I’m not immersed in code or equations, you’ll often find me dreaming up inventions, reading about scientific breakthroughs, or studying how technology impacts communities.

I don’t just write code—I design impact. Every project I build is a step toward a future where technology serves people, where discovery becomes transformation, and where no idea is too ambitious if it’s backed by vision and work.
